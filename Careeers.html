Career Services

Our Career Services team is dedicated to helping students and alumni succeed in their professional journeys. We offer a range of resources, from career counseling to job placement support.

Job Placement Assistance

We work closely with top employers to provide job placement opportunities for our students and graduates:

  • On-campus recruitment drives
  • Internship programs with leading companies
  • Career fairs and networking events

Career Counseling

Our experienced career counselors help students identify their strengths and guide them towards successful career paths:

  • Personalized career assessments and guidance
  • Resume building and interview preparation
  • Workshops on professional skills development

Employer Partnerships

We collaborate with industry leaders to create internship and full-time employment opportunities:

  • Tech Giants: Google, Microsoft, Amazon
  • Finance Leaders: Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan
  • Manufacturing & Engineering: Tesla, GE, Boeing
