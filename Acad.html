Admission Requirements

Below are the general admission criteria for each program:

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech): Candidates must have completed 12 years of schooling with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects and a minimum aggregate of 60%.
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA): Applicants must have completed high school (10+2) with at least 50% marks in any stream.
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech): Candidates must hold a B.Tech or equivalent degree in the relevant field with a minimum of 55% aggregate marks.

Student Life

Our campus offers a vibrant and inclusive environment, encouraging both academic and personal growth. Students can participate in various clubs, events, and initiatives:

  • Clubs and Societies: Choose from technical, cultural, and social clubs like the Coding Club, Music Society, or the Debate Team.
  • Sports Facilities: Engage in a range of sports activities with our well-equipped gym, basketball court, and cricket grounds.
  • Events and Workshops: Attend regular workshops, guest lectures, and cultural festivals to enhance your skills and network with industry professionals.
